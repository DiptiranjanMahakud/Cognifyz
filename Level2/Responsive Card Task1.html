Beneath the cerulean sky's grand dome, Beside the bay where foamy waves do comb, There stands a tree, a sentinel of time, Its boughs, like ancient poets, touch the sublime. With roots that weave through sand and stone, It whispers tales in a timeless tone, As the ocean's breath does kiss its leaves, In harmony, their secret stories interweave. Its branches reach for the heavens, yearning high, With leaves that dance beneath the azure sky, A guardian of the shore, in solitude it stands, A witness to the world's shifting sands. Its trunk, a testament to strength and grace, In nature's rhythm, it finds its place, The tree beside the bay, a timeless art, A living poem etched on the ocean's heart.
